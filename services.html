Services

Recruitment

Full cycle recruitment or any aspect of it. I can create the job posting and post the job in various places. I can recommend some low cost ways to advertise that is still effective. I can screen applications, conduct phone screens to narrow the pile even more, conduct face to face interviews or recommend good interview techniques. I can provide testing (skill or personality), and conduct reference and background checks. If your company is not currently using an applicant tracking system (a computer system that manages applications) and would like to learn more about them, I can assist with that as well.

Onboarding

Onboarding is more than just getting the required paperwork and desk and computer set up new employee on their first day. It is teaching them about the culture and expectations of the company. It is making them feel welcome. It is making sure there is someone that is guiding them and answering questions in the first few months of work. It is setting them up for success.

Training

My background in training is conducting employee orientations, teaching about company culture, and software training primarily in Microsoft Office. However, as long as I can learn the material that needs to be taught, I can certainly provide other types of training. Sometimes a group format is better but sometimes one of one training is better. You choose!

Employee Retention Strategies

There is a talent shortage in various industries. As more and more baby boomers retire, this will just get worse. Employees will jump to other jobs if they think things will be better there. The cost of recruiting and training new people is very expensive. That is why it is important for companies to have strategies to try and retain their current employees.

Business Process Analysis and Office Organization

Sometimes the business processes your company uses worked at the time they were started but as your company grows or changes, there may be a better way to do things. Some changes can make the work flow more efficient, therefore reducing costs or allowing you to do more.

Bookkeeping

Sometimes you can get bogged down doing bookkeeping for your business taking away time to actually make money in your business. If you need help with Accounts Receivable, Accounts Payable, Invoicing, Bank Reconciliations, Bank Deposits, GST, I can help!

Administrative work

This could include a full range of things including event planning, mail merges / customer mail outs, create brochures / manuals, maintain employee files, data entry, reports, purchasing, travel arrangements, customer service, or any other related tasks.

Payroll and Benefit Administration

I can assist managing or implementing a benefit program for you company. I can also assist doing payroll on a periodic or regular basis.

Research

Do you need help finding out more information about something that you may want to do or use but just don't have the time to do it yourself? I can help!
