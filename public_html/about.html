About

Janice Richardson is the sole proprietor of Business Perspectives and she has a Bachelor of Business Administration specializing in Human Resources from the University of Regina. She is also in the process of obtaining her CHRP (Canadian Human Resources Profession) designation. Janice has a broad spectrum of experience combining Human Resources and business. Her strengths are in recruitment, training, organization & planning but also has experience in bookkeeping, payroll, analytics, administrative work, research and writing. She has worked in many sectors including manufacturing, engineering, oil and gas, healthcare, non-profit, IT/web design, hospitality, and retail. Janice is always striving to help her clients improve their business processes resulting in more efficient or improved business practices. Janice is heavily involved in the community volunteering in non-profit organizations, church, sports teams, and schools. She has a heart for people with various disadvantages and participated in a home building mission trip in Mexico in March 2014.
