About

Hi. I am Janice Richardson and I am the sole proprietor of Business Perspectives. My experience in Human Reources and business has given me firsthand understanding of the work fluctuation that takes place and the stress that it causes. This inspired me to start up a small business offering companies temporary relief as projects pile up. With a Business Administration degree specialzing in Human Resources and training towards a CHRP designation, I am qualified to handle various tasks in the field. My greatest strength and passion is in the area of recruitment and training. I also have skills and experience in administrative work, onboarding, employee relations, payroll, benefits, planning events, bookkeeping, and research & writing. I am proficent in Microsoft Office programs and have worked on many database and custom-made programs.

My broad spectrum of experience combines Human Resources and business with working knowledge in sectors including manufacturing, engineering, oil & gas, senior care, non-profit, IT, publishing, hospitality and retail. I love working with people and I strive to help my clients improve their business processes. I am heavily involved in the community volunteering in a varity of charitable organizations, church, sports teams, and schools. I have also been on a Mission trip to Mexico to help build homes for the less fortunate.
